Resources to Jumpstart Your PM Career

PM Roadmap

Understand what product managers do, types of PMs (technical, growth, etc.), and explore job descriptions on platforms like LinkedIn, Indeed, and PM career blogs.

Learn product thinking, user research, wireframing, data analytics, and basic tech literacy. Consider tools like Figma, Google Analytics, SQL, and roadmap planning.

Highlight impact, cross-functional collaboration, and outcomes. Tailor your resume for PM keywords. Optimize your LinkedIn headline and About section for searchability.

Practice product sense, execution, estimation, metrics, and behavioral questions. Use frameworks like CIRCLES, STAR, and partner with mock interview buddies.

Track your job applications. Prioritize companies that fit your values and experience. Reach out to alumni or contacts for referrals.

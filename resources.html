Interview Prep

Use this 2-week guided plan to prepare for behavioral and product case interviews. Inspired by a top Reddit guide.

  • List common behavioral questions (Glassdoor, PM communities)
  • Write 8–10 detailed STAR stories from your experience
  • Rehearse and refine your answers
  • Memorize and practice with mock interviews

  • Read Decode and Conquer + Cracking the PM Interview
  • Master frameworks: Product sense, metrics, execution
  • Practice with and without guidance
  • Book mock interviews with peers or mentors
